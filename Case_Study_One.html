personal portfolio

This project explored the importance of considering content – in all its forms – as a core element of any piece of interaction design that can, itself, be designed. As content strategist Karen McGrane puts it: “Well-structured content is the backbone and starting point of all successful web designs and user interactions.” Tha aim of this project was to curate a selection of pieces of work that would explain who I am and my current capabilities both in design and development.
